# Task-1 Business Sales Performance Analytics
##Business sales Performance Analytics focuses on revenue trends, product performance, regional sales distribution, and business growth opportunities through data analysis and visualization

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn

## Dashboard Highlights
- Monthly & Weekly Revenue Trends
- Top 10 Products by Revenue
- Revenue by Category
- Revenue by Region
- Top 10 Cities by Revenue

## Key Insights
- Identified top-performing products and categories.
- Analyzed regional and city-wise sales performance.
- Tracked revenue trends and growth patterns.
- Generated actionable business recommendations.

## Outcome
Generated actionable business insights to support data-driven decision-making and identify growth opportunities.
